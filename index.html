Ahoy there, my name is Jason Quaccia, I'm new to the front-end scene but I'm learning ALL the time. Creative freedom is what I want and I am getting after it. Born and raised in the beautiful golden state just 30 minutes south of San Francisco, I am a native to the Bay Area landscape. I have been toying with creative thoughts on the web since April 2014. When I'm not behind a computer screen you can find me enjoying the cool mountain air, losing myself at an awesome concert or just unwinding with the latest Thomas Pynchon novel.

I received a certificate in front-end development after enrolling in a 3 month bootcamp course with Thinkful, an online school. Upon completion I was left in a very fortunate position having gained the fundamentals of HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript. Since Thinkful I have been hacking away just about everyday and have a plethora of projects and technologies I'd like to build and master this year. If you'd like to find out more about Thinkful and what it's all about, go ahead and flip my picture over on the left.
